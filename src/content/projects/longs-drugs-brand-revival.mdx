## Brief

Refresh the Longs Drugs brand and website in a way that respects local familiarity while making the system easier to use across digital, retail, and promotional touchpoints.

## Concept

The direction keeps the brand recognizable while tightening typography, hierarchy, image treatment, and page structure.

## System

The project can include identity rules, web page modules, promotional layouts, retail graphics, and digital navigation patterns.

## Outcome

This case study should show how an established brand can feel clearer and more current without losing the equity people already recognize.
