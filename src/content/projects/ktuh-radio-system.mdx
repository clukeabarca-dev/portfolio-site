## Brief

Create a website and apparel direction for KTUH Radio that can support schedules, programming, events, and station merchandise.

## Concept

The direction treats the station as a living cultural platform, with strong type, expressive imagery, and flexible apparel graphics.

## System

The project can include web modules, show pages, schedule logic, merch graphics, and campaign templates.

## Outcome

This case study should show how a radio brand can feel useful online and expressive in physical merchandise.
