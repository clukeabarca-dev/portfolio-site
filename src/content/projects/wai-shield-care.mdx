## Brief

Create a brand and packaging system for incontinence products that communicates care, trust, and product clarity.

## Concept

Wai Shield Care uses calm color, clear product hierarchy, and restrained typography to make sensitive product decisions easier.

## System

The project can include naming, packaging fronts, product-family color logic, claims hierarchy, and retail display behavior.

## Outcome

This page should show how packaging can be sensitive, useful, and visually confident at the same time.
