## Brief

Create branding and apparel for a Rotary Hawaii regional initiative that needs to work across events, groups, and printed materials.

## Concept

The direction focuses on recognition at a distance, clear event hierarchy, and apparel that feels unified without becoming overly formal.

## System

The project can include apparel graphics, event marks, print collateral, digital announcements, and badge-style identity elements.

This page should show how event branding can bring people together while staying practical to produce.
