## Brief

Create a visual identity and website system for Manoa Now that can support campus stories, editorial categories, and changing content needs.

## Concept

The identity treats the publication as an active editorial platform: flexible, organized, and built for regular publishing.

## System

The project can include a logo system, article templates, homepage modules, category treatments, and social graphics.

## Outcome

This page should show how identity and website structure can make a publication feel more coherent and easier to maintain.
