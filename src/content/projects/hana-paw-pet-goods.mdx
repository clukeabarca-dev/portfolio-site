## Brief

Build a pet-products brand that can work across packaging, retail displays, and customer-facing product information.

## Concept

Hana Paw uses a warm visual voice with clear packaging hierarchy so the brand feels friendly without becoming childish.

## System

The system can include product families, labels, icons, color coding, packaging fronts, and retail graphics.

## Outcome

This case study should show how personality and utility can work together in consumer packaging.
