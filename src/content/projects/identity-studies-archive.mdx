## Brief

Create a flexible page for multiple identity case studies that may not each need a full standalone product page.

## Concept

The archive treats identity work as a system of decisions: mark, type, color, imagery, and application.

## System

This page can hold several brand studies with short explanations, image sets, and application examples.

## Outcome

This project page should give smaller identity projects a strong place in the portfolio without overextending them.
