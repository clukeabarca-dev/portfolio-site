## Brief

Create a jewelry brand and packaging system that feels refined, intimate, and practical for retail or direct-to-consumer sales.

## Concept

The direction uses quiet typography, tactile materials, and close product imagery to create a sense of care and detail.

## System

Packaging, inserts, labels, photography, and digital product moments should all feel connected.

## Outcome

This case study should show how small-format packaging can carry a large brand impression.
