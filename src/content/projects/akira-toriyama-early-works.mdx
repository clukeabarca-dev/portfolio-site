## Brief

Create an exhibition identity concept for early works, using editorial systems, posters, and archival pacing.

## Concept

The direction focuses on energy, sequence, and visual storytelling while avoiding direct use of copyrighted character artwork in this scaffold.

## System

The project can include poster concepts, exhibition panels, publication spreads, wayfinding moments, and archive-style metadata.

## Outcome

This is presented as a self-initiated exhibition concept focused on archival energy, editorial pacing, and graphic storytelling.
