## Brief

Create a brand and packaging language for eco products that feels local, useful, and easy to navigate.

## Concept

The system uses natural color, simple packaging hierarchy, and product-family logic to avoid looking generic.

## System

Packaging, labels, iconography, product photography, and retail touchpoints should feel like one family.

## Outcome

This page should highlight the balance between sustainability cues and clear commercial packaging.
