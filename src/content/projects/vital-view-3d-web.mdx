## Brief

Design a website direction for Vital View 3D that explains a technical product clearly and builds trust quickly.

## Concept

The site uses structured content, precise visual hierarchy, and controlled imagery to make healthcare technology easier to understand.

## System

The project can include homepage modules, product explanation sections, feature pages, user pathways, and conversion points.

## Outcome

This case study should show how digital design can clarify a specialized product without overcomplicating the experience.
